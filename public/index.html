Heifer

Get the dairy fat you need from the dairies you have.

Here's the formula for calculating how much of two different milkfat dairies to combine to get a target milkfat. For the volume of higher milkfat dairy:

higher milkfat volume = (target milkfat percent - lower milkfat percent) / (higher milkfat percent - lower milkfat percent) * target volume

And then for the lower milkfat volume:

lower milkfat volume = target volume - higher milkfat volume

This calculator uses the following milkfat values:

36%

1%
